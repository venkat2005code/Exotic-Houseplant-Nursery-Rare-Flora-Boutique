Our Story

A passion for the rare, the weird, and the beautifully mutated.

Cultivating Rarity

Founded in 2018, the Rare Flora Boutique began as a small, specialized greenhouse dedicated to the preservation and cultivation of highly variegated and mutated aroids. What started as a personal collection rapidly grew into a premier destination for serious plant collectors worldwide.

Our mission is not just to sell plants, but to educate. We believe that caring for exotic specimens requires a deep understanding of their natural habitats. Our team of expert botanists works tirelessly to provide you with the most robust plant care profiles and diagnostics.

Every specimen that leaves our nursery is thoroughly inspected, properly acclimated, and guaranteed to arrive in pristine condition.
