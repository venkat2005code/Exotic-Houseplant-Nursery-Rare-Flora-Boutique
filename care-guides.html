Care Guides

Expert botanical knowledge for your rare plant collection.

Monstera Deliciosa Albo

Requires high humidity (60%+) and bright, indirect light to maintain white variegation without burning. Use an airy, chunky aroid mix.

Anthurium Warocqueanum

Needs consistent moisture but must never sit in water. Sphagnum moss or tree fern fiber works best. Aim for 75%+ humidity.

Pest Management Basics

Spider Mites

Look for fine webbing. Increase humidity and thoroughly wipe down leaves with a diluted neem oil or insecticidal soap solution every 5 days for 3 weeks.

Thrips

Isolate the plant immediately. Use systemic granules in the soil (if not using predatory mites) and a targeted contact spray on the foliage.

Mealybugs

Look for white cottony masses. Spot treat immediately with a cotton swab dipped in 70% isopropyl alcohol, checking thoroughly in all stem crevices.
