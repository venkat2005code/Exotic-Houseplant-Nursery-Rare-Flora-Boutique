Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship globally with the proper CITES and phytosanitary documentation.

How do I care for my Monstera Albo?

Variegated Monsteras need bright, indirect light and a well-draining chunky aroid mix. Let the soil dry out partially between waterings.

What is your live arrival guarantee?

We guarantee live, healthy arrival on all shipments. Due to the sensitive nature of our specimens, if there are any issues, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery.

Can I visit the nursery in person?

Our primary greenhouse is open to the public by appointment only. Please use the booking form above to schedule a VIP consultation.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for registered botanical retailers and bulk tissue culture orders. Reach out to our team directly to establish a partnership.
